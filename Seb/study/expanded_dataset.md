# Expanded two-panel dataset across the GOE

**Compiled:** 2026-07-12. Extends the Tumbiana–Gunflint pilot (`../pilot/compiled_dataset.md`) to a
6-formation chert-preservation panel plus a cross-GOE bulk-redox (shale) panel. Fiction-free: every
entry sourced; "?" = not found in the open literature during this compilation (gap, not estimate).

## Panel A — Chert-hosted biotas (preservation / maturity / mineralization)

Ordinal scores: morphological fidelity 0 (none/mat-only)–3 (diverse, diagnostic); silicification
0–3; pyritisation 0–3. Qualitative; for hypothesis-generation, not statistics.

| Formation | Age (Ga) | GOE | Thermal maturity (RSCM/grade) | Morph. fidelity | Silicification | Pyritisation | Key source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Strelley Pool | ~3.4 | pre | disordered CM, Raman ~300 °C | **3** (lenticular; chains/clusters; tail-like) | **3** (silicified chert) | low | Delarue2020; Delarue2021; Sugitani2010; GPL1817 |
| Moodies Gp. | ~3.22 | pre | low-grade (Barberton) | **1** (tufted mats, cavity-dwelling; cell-scale limited) | 1 (silicified/dolomitized mounds) | ? | Homann2015,2016 |
| Tumbiana Fm. | ~2.72 | pre | weakly metamorphosed, <300 °C | **0–1** (rare putative filaments; abundant OM globules) | 1 (chert layers) | 1 (micropyrites, Fe-respiration) | Lepot2009; Decreane2021 |
| Belcher Gp. | ~2.0 | post | low-grade; "best-preserved Orosirian" | **3** (Belcher Group Microbiota, diverse) | **3** (chert-replaced stromatolites in dolostone) | ? | Érudit2024; Hodgskiss&Sperling2020 |
| Gunflint Fm. | ~1.88 | post | prehnite–pumpellyite; Schreiber least metam. | **3** (Gunflintia, Huroniospora, Animikiea…) | **3** (early silicification, encapsulation) | 2 (patchy; locally sulfate-limited; destructive) | Lepot2017; Alleon2016; Wacey2013 |
| Sokoman IF | ~1.88 | post | low-grade (Labrador Trough) | **3** (Gunflint-type, indistinguishable) | 2 (chert in BIF) | ? | Science1981 (211:478) |

**Pattern in Panel A:** morphological fidelity tracks **silicification**, not GOE side. Pre-GOE
Strelley Pool is morphology-rich where silica-encapsulated; pre-GOE Tumbiana/Moodies are poor where
not. Post-GOE Gunflint/Sokoman/Belcher are silica-rich and morphology-rich. Pyritisation is absent
or *destructive* in the best-preserved biotas.

## Panel B — Bulk-redox shales across the GOE (Fe-speciation / sulfate)

| Unit | Age (Ga) | GOE | FeHR/FeT, Fe_py/FeHR (qual.) | Redox reading | Key source |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Jeerinah Fm. | ~2.7 | pre | pyrite-bearing; pyrite-formation pathway studied | complex / ferruginous | UNIL2023 (pyrite pathways) |
| Mt McRae Shale | ~2.5 | pre | detailed spec exists; variable; pyrite present | spatial/temporal variation; transient "whiffs" | Raiswell2011; Anbar2007; Kakegawa1998 |
| Barney Creek (McArthur) | ~1.64 | post | FeHR/FeT elevated; euxinic intervals | photic-zone euxinia (Brocks-2005 hopanes) | Sperling2014 |
| **Compilation finding** | A–P | both | ferruginous dominant overall; **BUT** diagenesis reassessment finds **no statistically significant FeHR/FeT or Fe_py/FeHR trend across the Archean–Proterozoic transition** | **contested** | Poulton&Canfield2011; Planavsky2011; Sperling2014; GCA diagenesis reassessment (Archimer 95949) |

**Pattern in Panel B:** the "redox regime shifted cleanly across the GOE" premise is **not** securely
established at the bulk-shale level — ferruginous conditions dominate on both sides, euxinia is
spatially variable, and a diagenesis-aware reassessment finds no significant A–P trend.

## Data gaps (honest)
1. No published RSCM peak-T for Tumbiana or Moodies (mineralogical bounds only); Strelley Pool and
   Gunflint have Raman/grade constraints. New RSCM on Tumbiana/Moodies needed for a strict match.
2. No bulk Poulton–Canfield FeHR/FeT for the chert biotas (existing Fe work is nanoscale); Panel B
   uses shales, which lack the microfossil record — the two panels cannot yet be merged per-sample.
3. Pyritisation scores for Moodies/Belcher/Sokoman are unknown ("?") — would need new petrography.
